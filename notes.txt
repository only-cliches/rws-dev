https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/HTTP/Headers/Content-Security-Policy

content security policy
default-src: catch all
child-src: webworkers
connect-src: fetch and <a> links
font-src: @font-source css
frame-src: iframes
img-src: images and photos
media-src: audio and video html tags
object-src <embed>, <object> and <applet> tags (set to "none")
script-src: <script> sources
style-src: css sources
form-action: self
base-uri: self
